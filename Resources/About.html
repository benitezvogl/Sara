S.A.R.A. - (synesthetic augmented reality application) is an App exploring the potential of using a mobile device as a unique musical interface via a digitally augmented synesthetic experience.

S.A.R.A. translates the surrounding environment into sounds based on the camera input and the movement of the device. It uses generative synthesis to produce the sound, please check the help section on how to load your own pure data patches.
